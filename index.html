Welcome to Maisha Yangu Organization

Maisha Yangu Organization is a community-based organization working with children and youth in Nairobi slums.

We use art and education projects to help kids & youths share ideas and stories with their peers , community, and decision-makers. We empower them to excel in their education, life skill, character and leadership. Through education, art and mentorship, we have provided them with valuable structured programs that keep them away from negative vices while discovering their gifts and talents without much limits.

What other impact is there?

“In form 3, I almost dropped out of school because of lack of school fees. My mother had no money. Even to find money to feed us and pay for our school fees was hard. So I had no option but to stay at home. Maisha Yangu Organization has really supported me and now I’m happy. I’m waiting for my annual exams.” Irene (beneficiary of our programs).

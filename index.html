Welcome to Maisha Yangu Organization

Maisha Yangu Organization is a community-based organization working with children and youth in Nairobi slums.

We use art and education projects to help kids & youths share ideas and stories with their peers, community, and decision-makers. We empower them to excel in their education, life skills, character, and leadership. Through education, art, and mentorship, we have provided them with valuable structured programs that keep them away from negative vices while discovering their gifts and talents without many limits.
